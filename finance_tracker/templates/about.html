About ExpenseStats

ExpenseStats is a data-driven personal finance application designed to help users track, analyze, and understand their spending patterns.

It provides clear insights through structured data, visual charts, and intelligent summaries, enabling better financial decisions.

With a focus on simplicity and precision, ExpenseStats transforms raw expense data into meaningful information that empowers users to manage their finances effectively.
